# 🚀 LPU Auto Login | Kinetic Obsidian V3.0

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-f68220.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)
[![LPU](https://img.shields.io/badge/LPU-Student_Project-blue.svg)](https://www.lpu.in)

**LPU Auto Login** is a premium, high-performance Chrome Extension designed for students of Lovely Professional University. Say goodbye to the daily hassle of logging into multiple portals manually.

---

## ✨ Features

- **🛡️ Kinetic Obsidian UI**: A next-gen, neumorphic (3D) dark theme designed for performance and aesthetics.
- **⚡ Smart UMS Automation**: Sophisticated logic for `ums.lpu.in/lpuums`. It automatically fills your ID, waits for the site to verify your registration, and then injects your password.
- **🌐 Internet Portal Booster**: Instant login for `internet.lpu.in` and `myaccountinternet.lpu.in` (handles auto-checking terms and `@lpu.com` appending).
- **🎓 MyClass Integrated**: Unified automation for the MyClass portal with shared UMS credentials.
- **🌗 Dual-Theme Support**: Toggle between a sleek light mode and a deep obsidian dark mode.
- **🔒 Privacy First**: All credentials are stored locally on your machine using `chrome.storage.local`. NO data leaves your browser.

---

## 🛠️ Installation

1.  **Download this repository** as a ZIP or clone it using:
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/rohit124551/LPU-Auto-Login.git
    ```
2.  Open **Google Chrome** and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  Enable **"Developer mode"** (top right corner).
4.  Click **"Load unpacked"** and select the folder where you cloned/extracted the extension.
5.  Click the extension icon in your toolbar, enter your credentials, and hit **LOCK PORTAL**.
