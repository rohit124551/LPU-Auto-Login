# 🛡️ Privacy Policy | LPU Auto Login

**Last Updated: April 3, 2026**

Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how **LPU Auto Login** handles your data.

### 1. Data Collection & Usage
LPU Auto Login does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers. All user data, including Registration IDs and Passwords, is stored exclusively in the user's local browser storage (`chrome.storage.local`).

### 2. Purpose of Data
The stored data is used solely for the purpose of automating the login process on LPU portals:
- `internet.lpu.in` (Internet Gateway)
- `myclass.lpu.in` (UMS / MyClass)
- `oas.lpu.in` (OAS Portal)
- `lovelyprofessionaluniversity.codetantra.com` (Codetantra sessions)

The extension does not track browsing history, analyze user behavior, or interact with any non-LPU websites.

### 3. Data Security
User credentials remain on the user's device. No third party, including the developer, has access to this data. For your security, the extension also masks your passwords in the popup interface unless you are actively typing them.

### 4. Third-Party Services
The extension does not use third-party analytics, advertisements, or tracking scripts. No trackers are embedded in the extension.
